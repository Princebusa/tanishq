What is a Diamond Cut?

The cut of a natural diamond determines how it interacts with light. The Cut Grading System assesses diamond appearance, including its brightness, fire, and scintillation, which make up the way a diamond reflects light, dispersing it into color, and sparkles.

What is Diamond Color ?

Diamond color denotes the presence or absence of hue, ranging from colorless to light yellow or brown. The scale typically ranges from D (colorless) to Z (light yellow or brown), with colorless diamonds allowing maximum light reflection, thereby increasing their brilliance and value.

Small Inclusions

How is carat measured?

Carat weight measures a diamond's mass, with one carat equaling 200 milligrams or 0.2 grams. It's a standardized unit used to determine the size and weight of a diamond, influencing its value and rarity.
